Top 10 Most Powerful Abilities in Jujutsu Kaisen

  1. Hollow Purple

    2. Gojo Satoru's most powerful extension of the Limitless technique,Hollow Purple , is one of his most powerful attacks. It is a combination of Amplification Lapse Blue and Reversal Red . Blue is the amplification of Limitless, and it brings the concept of negative space to reality and pulls everything towards Blue with a magnetic-like effect. Red is the reversal that pushes everything back using Reversed Cursed Technique (positive energy generated by multiplying cursed energy by cursed energy). The combination forms Imaginary Technique Hollow Purple. By forcing negative space (Blue) and excess space (Red) together, it creates an imaginary mass that erases anything it comes into contact with. This occurs at the atomic level and can destroy cities, buildings, cursed spirits , and sorcerers with ease. Hollow Purple managed to nearly finish off Hanami , a special-grade cursed spirit, while destroying an entire forest. In his battle with Toji Fushiguro , he used the technique to kill the infamous sorcerer killer who is beyond special grade. The last time he used Purple was against Sukuna , and the technique decimated Shinjuku and heavily injured the King of Curses .Â 

  2. Malevolent Shrine

    3. Malevolent Shrine is one of the most powerful Domain Expansions in Jujutsu Kaisen . Unlike other domains, Malevolent Shrine does not enclose itself within a barrier ; rather, it doesn't have one at all. This creates a binding vow ; by allowing the target to escape, Sukuna can expand his domain to a maximum radius of 200 meters. The domain, like others, has a sure-hit effect and imbues the user's cursed technique into the domain. The techniques Sukuna uses for his sure-hit effect are Cleave and Dismantle. Cleave is a precision-based attack that dices the target, while Dismantle is a powerful slash that is great for pure destruction. Within the domain, Sukuna sets up everything that has cursed energy to get attacked by Cleave. Meanwhile, everything that doesn't have cursed energy, like buildings, is targeted by Dismantle. This allows Sukuna to slash everything within the radius of the domain. This is incredibly powerful and managed to create a crator in Shibuya .Â 

  3. World Cutting Slash

    4. The World Cutting Slash is an extension technique of Sukuna's Dismantle. Normally, Dismantle sends a black blade of cursed energy to slice anything. However, with Mahoraga's adaptation, Sukuna learned how to expand his attack to target the entire world, existence itself. What that means is that instead of attacking a specific object or person, it targets the space the person is in. It is like if two stick figures were fighting on a 2D piece of paper and someone cut it with scissors. Even if the 2D figures had protection against attacks, it wouldn't matter as the scissors would cut the paper they were in. The World Cutting Slash works the same way and can cut cursed techniques . This single attack managed to bypass Gojo Satoru's neutral Limitless , which creates infinite distance between himself and objects. In order to perform this powerful technique, Sukuna must perform hand signs and chants. He used this attack again against Hajime Kashimo , and it created a slash that sliced a large portion of land. The only reason he didn't have to make hand signs and chants when he used the technique to kill Gojo Satoru was because he made a binding vow . This binding vow allowed Sukuna to kill Gojo without needing hand signs or chants, but in return, he is now required to perform more hand signs and chants for each World Cutting Slash he performs now.Â 

  4. Infinite Void

    5. Infinite Void is Gojo Satoru's Domain Expansion . It is similar to other domains as it closes itself within a barrier and has a sure-hit effect. What makes Infinite Void so powerful is its ability to stun any opponent once in the domain. Gojo does this by overloading the target's brain with tons of information, making them incapable of moving or attacking. This limitless stream of information makes it impossible for the target to perform cursed techniques , Simple Domain , Domain Expansion, or anything else. Even after a few seconds of being inside Infinite Void, you absorb a year's worth of information. This is an extremely powerful Domain Expansion, as it makes any opponent inside a vegetable for Gojo Satoru to destroy, making the enemy completely defenseless. The only people that aren't affected by this ability are Gojo himself and anyone he touches. Not only this, but the domain is one of the most refined in Jujutsu Kaisen , and its only rival is Malevolent Shrine . For example, during his fight with Jogo (a Special-Grade cursed spirit), Gojo easily overpowered his domain with his own. This ability can also work on multiple individuals at once. Even Sukuna admitted how strong Infinite Void was.Â 

  5. 10 Shadows

    6. The 10 Shadows Technique is a cursed technique inherited by the Zenin Clan . It is powerful due to its versatility and adaptability. Currently, Megumi Fushiguro uses it as his primary way of defeating cursed spirits. This ability allows the user to store Shikigami inside shadows and release them at any time. There are a total of 10 Shikigami that a sorcerer can obtain. A bearer of the 10 Shadows has two Divine Dogs at birth and must defeat other Shikigami in order to tame them. Once tamed, the user can spawn them at any time and have full control over them. This can be used for offense or defense, and shadows can also store cursed tools and people. What makes this technique truly terrifying is the 10th shadow, Divine General Mahoraga . This one Shikigami was so powerful that no one in the history of Jujutsu Kaisen has ever successfully tamed it. Mahoraga's ability is to adapt to any and all phenomena. Not only this, but his physical strength is immeasurable, and his large size makes him an intimidating opponent. He was able to keep up with a 15-finger Sukuna and adapt to his cursed technique. He is shown to be able to throw buildings, cars, and other large physical objects with ease. His speed and explosive strength are incredible and can easily overwhelm a weaker opponent. His adpatation works through his eight-handled wheel that spins as the fight progresses. This occurs when Mahoraga is attacked, and his wheel spins. If the opponent uses the same attack again, he will counter it. At some point, Mahoraga would completely be immune to that attack. The Shikigami also has the Sword of Extermination , a blade that is coated with positive energy . This makes it a great weapon for cursed spirits , as they die instantly if hit with pure positive energy. It is also especially hard to kill, as he heals quickly through adaptation. However, for more complicated techniques, the wheel must spin more times. After Sukuna took over Megumi's body, he used Mahoraga to adapt to Gojo's Limitless (a technique that creates infinite distance) and effectively killed the strongest sorcerer of the modern era.Â 

  6. Cleave

    7. Cleave is part of Sukuna's cursed technique . It makes serveral slashes that dice the opponent swiftly like an onion. The slashes occur instantly as the technique is activated, and the resulting outcome cuts the enemy down into small cubes. The slashes adjust the cursed energy output to cut the enemy down in one fell swoop. In order to activate this technique, Sukuna must touch his opponent with his hand. It is powerful enough to cut up strong sorcerers like Ryu and Gojo Satoru's . His extension of this technique is called Spiderweb, and Sukuna touches the ground and cuts it up in the pattern of a spiderweb.Â 

  7. Dismantle

    8. Dismantle is the second part of Sukuna's cursed technique . It is less powerful than Cleave, but it causes more destruction and damage. Dismantle is an invisible slash made of cursed energy that is sent directly towards an object. The slash is incrediblily fast so it isn't easy to dodge. Sukuna can send these slashes in any direction or angle, and can adjust the size to make the cut thin or large. It is simple but can sever skyskrapers and buildings with ease like butter. When used in his domain , Dismantle has the power to delete an entire city in one second. The slash can cut almost anything and has such a high energy output that it creates shockwaves in the air. Sukuna can send multiple of these slashes at once and decimate everything in its path. This was shown in the Shibuya Arc where he sliced up multiple buildings throughout his fight between Jogo and Mahoraga .

  8. Heavenly Restriction

    9. Heavenly Restriction is someone who has no cursed energy . Normally, everyone has some amount of cursed energy, even non-sorcerers. However, people born with Heavenly Restriction have zero, and due to this, they cannot use cursed energy or cursed techniques . Although this sounds terrible in the world of Jujutsu Kaisen , where cursed energy and techniques mean everything in battles, having no cursed energy means your physical prowess, strength, and capabilities are massively increased to superhuman levels. A binding vow makes it so that there is balance; in exchange for zero cursed energy, you have immense strength and speed. We can see how powerful Heavenly Restriction is because of Toji Fushiguro . His job is to kill strong sorcerers , and he does this by using cursed tools . Toji is able to keep up and outclass many cursed users and sorcerers alike. His feats include being able to keep up with a teen Gojo Satoru and surprise attack him, being able to jump into high buildings, chopping someone's head off with his fist, and being as fast as a 5-finger Sukuna . Toji also managed to outpower and defeat Dagon (A special grade cursed spirit) inside of his own Domain Expansion with ease using Playful Cloud (A Special Grade cursed tool). After Maki Zenin's awakening, she had her cursed energy reserves to zero and became on par with Toji in physical strength and power.

  9. Neutral Limitless

    10. The neutral state of Limitless lets Gojo Satoru create infinite distance between himself and his opponents. He does this by taking a finite amount of space and dividing it infinitely to create infinite distance in a finite space. The physical appearnce is a dome shape and trasparent. An object trying to reach Gojo would never do so, as there is an infinite amount of distance between the object and him. This acts as a shield to protect him from attacks. What makes it so powerful is that it doesn't matter how powerful an attack is; it will never reach Gojo. For example, if you denoted the largest atomic bomb right next to him, it wouldn't hit him at all due to his technique. The energy, heat, and radiation would all be stopped by Infinity. He can control when it activates and deactivates and what things to let through or not. He does this by determining the object's speed, mass, size, cursed energy, and if it's dangerous using the Six Eyes . With Reversed Cursed Technique , he is able to keep his Infinity on and automatically stop any threats from hitting him without activating it manually. Things like air and light are let through, but if a bullet tries to hit him, his Infinity will activate and stop the bullet. This creates an effective defense mechanism and allows him to never be caught off guard.

  10. Fire Arrow

    11. The Fire Arrow is a part of Sukuna's cursed technique and can be activated by chanting "Open." When he does this, he can freely control flames and fire and form an arrow shape with his hand. This arrow can then be released and cause devastating damage. It is more powerful than Jogo's flames, a Special Grade cursed spirit with fire being his cursed technique. Its destructive capability is high, and when released, it creates an enormous pillar of fire that burns everything inside of it. This pillar can be very wide and high to cause maximum damage. Even Mahoraga could not survive this powerful attack despite his adapation ability. Sukuna can also use the Fire Arrow when inside his Domain Expansion in addition to his Dismantle and Cleave.
